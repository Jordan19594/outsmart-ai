Personalisation Hooks
Hook 1 — Upgrade, Not Convince

Barbara already sells RLT in three formats — she has bought into the modality commercially. The Solis SUPREME 2.0 pitch is not "here's a new concept" but "here's the professional evolution of what you already offer." The £25 Ultraslim add-on and £50 Dermaulux session become a £150–200/session standalone half-body protocol. Same belief, five times the revenue per treatment.

Hook 2 — ROI Is Self-Evident

At Bodyvie's current Reveallux pricing of £150/session, a Solis SUPREME 2.0 at £899 pays for itself in 6 sessions. That's under 2 weeks at 3 sessions per week. Zero consumables, no staff certification required, and the clinical irradiance exceeds their existing LED devices. Position this as the highest ROI capital investment in their equipment portfolio this year.

Hook 3 — Pioneer Identity Alignment

Bodyvie markets itself as "utilising only the latest technologies" and describes Barbara as a pioneer. The Solis SUPREME 2.0 is award-winning (lead with the award name), and full-body RLT panels are what premium Harley Street clinics began adding in 2024–2025. Frame it as: "Richmond should have what W1 has" — this fits Barbara's stated positioning of delivering Harley Street quality locally.

Likely Objections & Counters
Objection 1 — "We already have LED covered"

Dermaulux and Reveallux are small localised devices. The SUPREME 2.0 delivers half-body coverage at clinical irradiance in a single 10–20 min session — a different clinical proposition (systemic photobiomodulation, full-body anti-inflammatory, collagen at scale). Different use case, different patient journey, and a higher per-session fee warranted.

Objection 2 — Capital spend

At £899, this is the lowest-cost device Bodyvie has ever purchased relative to what it generates. Compare: Dermalux Flex MD retails at £3,500+. Endolift laser equipment: £30,000+. The ROI case is unusually clear — present it as a spreadsheet, not a pitch.

Objection 3 — Room allocation

Single-clinic Richmond site with a busy treatment schedule. The SUPREME 2.0 can operate as a session add-on (post-Endolift recovery, post-Endermologie, or standalone express session) rather than requiring a dedicated room block. It pairs naturally with their existing body treatment menu.
