### Section Header

> Something Has Changed — and Most People Haven't Caught Up

### Body Copy

Scammers used to be easy to spot. Bad grammar. Clumsy stories. Foreign phone numbers.

Not anymore.

In 2026, artificial intelligence lets criminals clone a loved one's voice from a 3-second social media clip. It generates phishing emails so polished that even cybersecurity professionals get fooled. It builds fake bank websites that look identical to the real thing.

Last year, Americans over 60 lost $4.8 billion to online scams. That number is growing by 30% every year.

The problem isn't that people aren't smart enough. The problem is that these scams are designed by machines — and they're evolving faster than most people realize.

## SECTION 10: OBJECTION HANDLING / FAQ

### Section Header

> Questions? We Have Answers.

**Q: I already get scam warnings from my bank. How is this different?**
A: Bank alerts are generic and reactive — they tell you after something happens. Outsmart AI is proactive. We teach you to recognize AI-specific threats like voice cloning, deepfake video, and AI-generated phishing before they reach you. Every issue includes step-by-step protection actions, not just warnings.

**Q: Is this only for older adults?**
A: No. AI scams target everyone. But our guides are designed to be clear, accessible, and actionable for people of all experience levels — including those who didn't grow up with technology. Many subscribers are adult children who read it alongside their parents.

**Q: I'm not very tech-savvy. Will I understand this?**
A: Yes. We write at a level that anyone can follow, with plain language, large text, and step-by-step screenshots. If a 12-year-old can understand it, we've done our job. No jargon. No assumptions.

**Q: Can I print it out?**
A: Absolutely. Every issue is designed to look great printed on regular paper. Many readers keep a printed copy by the phone or on the fridge.

**Q: What if I want to cancel?**
A: Cancel anytime with one click. No phone calls, no runaround. If you're not finding value, we don't want your money.

**Q: Can I gift this to my parents?**
A: Yes. The Annual Plan includes one gift subscription. The Family Bundle includes three subscriptions you can share with anyone.
